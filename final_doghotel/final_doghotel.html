Staying Overnight in our Luxury Suites
The Dog Hotel combines luxury, home-like amenities in a contemporary space. Each suite includes a custom metal frame lifted an inch off the ground with metal slats for optimal comfort, along with an orthopedic mattress. If you choose to not bring a blanket for your pet, we will provide a clean, cozy blanket. Lights are out at 8:30pm which means the classical music turns on, and the lights are dimmed. Hydrant Pet Hotel offers the best in dog boarding amenities and accommodations along with 24/7 staff supervision tending to your dogs personal needs at all hours. Our goal is to provide an upscale, comfortable place for your pet to stay while you are away.
